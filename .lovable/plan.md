
# Alumina Engineering — Modern Website Redesign

## Overview
A fresh, modern, professional website for Alumina Engineering Pvt. Ltd., an architectural products supplier based in Mumbai. The redesign will feature a sleek, contemporary look with smooth animations and a clean layout.

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero Section**: Full-width hero with a bold headline ("Engineering Excellence for Iconic Architecture"), a tagline, and a CTA button ("Explore Our Solutions"). Background with a subtle gradient or architectural imagery placeholder.
- **Trusted Brands Carousel**: Scrolling logo strip of principal brands (Dorma, Drainvac, FunderMax, Geggus, Skyfold, McKeon) with a subtle animation.
- **Mission / Vision / Values**: Three elegant cards with icons, each showing the mission, vision, and values text from the original site.
- **Principals/Products Overview**: Tabbed section showcasing each brand (Dorma, Skyfold, FunderMax, McKeon, Drainvac, Geggus) with description text and a placeholder image area.
- **Trusted Clients**: Logo grid of key clients (Oberoi, Reliance, Taj, Hiranandani, Hyatt).
- **Contact CTA Banner**: A call-to-action section with contact details and a "Get in Touch" button.

### 2. About Page
- Company overview paragraph
- Mission, Vision, Values in a visual layout

### 3. Products Page
- Grid of product categories (one card per brand) with descriptions
- Each card links to a detail view with more info

### 4. Contact Page
- Company address, phone numbers, email
- A contact form (name, email, phone, message)
- Embedded map placeholder

### 5. Navigation & Footer
- **Navbar**: Clean top navigation with logo placeholder, links (Home, About, Products, Principals, Services, Clients, Events, Contact), and a CTA button.
- **Footer**: Company info, quick links, contact details, social links.

## Design Direction
- **Color scheme**: Deep navy/dark blue primary with warm gold/amber accents on white background — professional and premium feel
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, large headings, generous whitespace
- **Animations**: Fade-in on scroll, smooth hover effects on cards
- **Fully responsive** for mobile, tablet, and desktop
